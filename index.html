Feed

Shrimps

Pellets are artificial feed for vaname shrimp in the form of tubes with different levels of hardness. In the pellets, there are usually special nutrients to accelerate the growth of shrimp. Starting from protein, carbohydrates, fat, fiber, and other important nutrients. And we have many different companies for shrimp feed.

Fish

As ingredients in aquaculture feed, fishmeal and fish oil supply essential amino acids and fatty acids reflected in the normal diet of fish. Fish oil is a major natural source of the healthy omega-3 fatty acids eicosapentaenoic acid (EPA) and docosahexaenoic acid (DHA).

Crab

Crabs are daily fed with trash fish, brackish water clams or boiled chicken waste @ 5-8% of their body weight. If the feed is given twice a day, major share must be given during evening hours.

Diseases and Preventions

Diseases affecting fish, shrimp, and crabs in aquaculture systems, along with the preventive measures to mitigate their impact. In fish farming, bacterial, viral, and parasitic infections pose significant challenges.
